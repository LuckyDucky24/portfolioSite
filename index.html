ABOUT ME

Hi! Thanks for taking a look. I am a self-taught Web Developer in Los Angeles. Making websites and web applications using JavaScript brings me great satisfaction.

Playing with computers has always been fun for me, whether it is building one from its separate parts or playing with the software.

I believe that programming is more than just a computer language to build web-sites and applications. It is also a tool to show how people think and can aid in understanding and solving human problems.

I completed a certification for Front-End Web Development through Udacity. This curriculum was created with AT&T, Google, and GitHub. I also have solid experience in sales.

Other than programming I like to read, ride my reliable 10+ year old Schwinn road bike, experience the outdoors, and listen to other people's stories.
